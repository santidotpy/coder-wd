World Cup 2022: Full list of groups and matches in Qatar

The 2022 World Cup is being played across eight venues in Qatar, which is the first Middle Eastern country to host the tournament. This year's location heavily impacts two important timing aspects for the tournament.

FIFA World Cup 2022 Stadiums

Al Bayt Stadium

Capacity: 60,000

Distance from Doha city centre: 27 miles

Designer: Dar Al-Handasah

Games: Eight, including the opening match

Lusail Stadium

Capacity: 80,000

Distance from Doha city centre: 10 miles

Designer: Foster + partners

Games: Ten, including the final

Al Janoub Stadium

Capacity: 40,000

Distance from Doha city centre: 14 miles

Designer: Dame Zaha Hadid

Games: Seven, up to last 16

Ahmad Bin Ali Stadium

Capacity: 40,000

Distance from Doha city centre: 14 miles

Designer: Ramboll

Games: Seven, up to last 16

Education City Stadium

Capacity: 40,000

Distance from Doha city centre: 7 miles

Designer: FIA Fenwick Iribarren Architects

Games: Eight, up to quarter-final

Education City Stadium

Capacity: 40,000

Distance from Doha city centre: 8 miles

Designer: Arab Engineering Bureau

Games: Eight, up to quarter-final

Stadium 974

Capacity: 40,000

Distance from Doha city centre: 6 miles

Designer: FIA Fenwick Iribarren Architects

Games: Seven, up to last 16

Khalifa International Stadium

Capacity: 45,416

Distance from Doha city centre: 8 miles

Games: Eight, including third-place play-off
